The Inspiration




There are days when it would be just too much of a struggle just to keep ourselves motivated and productive until the end of the day, let alone finding the urge to just get out of bed. With these days while in the amidst of a bloody pandemic, every single thing is being restricted to an certain length and requiring us to change and adapt to the new norm. In order to overcome these obstacles - at least for me - I would just need a bit of musical boost and a game changing go-to playlist just like what I have listed down there. I treat music as a sort of therapy or getaway. To calm my nerves, maintain my sanity, to overcome my traumas. Because music is not just notes, and chords, and lyrics, and melody, and tempo all combined to be one melodious jumble, it is much more than just that. It might not save the world, but it could save someone's world.
